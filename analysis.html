Objectives

The purpose of this document is to investigate the electricity usage at Terra Nova. At the time of writing a smart meter has not been installed because of the age of the oil boiler. I fear that it will no longer work after the installation. I do have records of monthly usage and costs continuously from July 2016.

This will be an ongoing project with new objectives being identified as the current analysis continues. I know that the electricity usage has declined since the records began. The current objectives are as follows:

  1. To determine the savings in kilo Watt hours (kWh) consumed over the last six years and to make a prediction of future demand;
  2. To set targets for future savings;
  3. To assess if changing supplier would result in future cost savings;
  4. Attempt to determine what appliances use in the daily activities.

Data storage

The electricity readings are added to an Calc spreadsheet where the charges are calculated. This spreadsheet is used to reconcile the bills that I receive from Ecotricity Ltd, my current supplier. The readings are taken first thing on the day following the last day of the month. A second reading is taken on the 7th day of each month and sent to my supplier for billing purposes.

The data is then added to a PostgreSQL database where the figures are directly downloaded for this analysis. A sample of the raw data is shown below:

The plot above clearly shows a continuous reduction in electricity usage over the period 2016 - 2022. The overall reduction is calculated thus:

reduction = (usage 2022 - usage 2016) / usage 2016
          = (2282 - 3687) / 3687
          = -0.38 or -38%

The above statistics are very interesting. The only year, in this analysis, for which there was an increase in usage was in 2017 with 1% over the 2016 total. The best year was 2020 with a 18% reduction; this was the year when a new washing machine was purchased. The preceding year saw the purchase of a rotary line and a Bosch refrigerator (September 2019). This may be why a 7.2% reduction in usage occurred in 2019; the third highest reduction. In May 2022 a new Bosch freezer was purchased which may account for the 7.9% reduction that year.

For 2022 compared for the same months January to October in 2021 the reduction in usage works out at -16.2%.

Forecast for 2022 November and December

Based on a 16.2% reduction in electricity usage for the year up to October I have calculated the usage for November and December as follows:

  • November = 240 x -16.2% = 201 kWh
  • December = 232 x -16.2% = 194 kWh

Costs

The amount charged by energy companies was usually in line with consumption until the end of 2021 when for some inexplicable reason costs went through the roof. This is especially perplexing as I have been with Ecotricity for over twenty years and all my electricity is derived from renewables. The unit cost of green energy has been going down for years so why unit costs are going up is an anomaly in a country with so called free market; a rigged market is a more appropriate description.
